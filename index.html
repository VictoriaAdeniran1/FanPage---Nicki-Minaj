Who is Nicki Minaj?

Information

Onika Tanya Maraj Nicki Minaj is a female rapper that debuted her firt album Pink Friday in 2010. She is 40 now and is known for her extensive rap career, funky and wild personas namely Roman Lewinsky and much more. She has great links with many other entertainers and rappers as she has over 50 different collabs with other artists. She was in the record label set up by rapper Lil Wayne called Young Money alongside rappers like Drake and Tyga. Just this year, she has set up her own record label HeavyOnIt where she hopes to emulate the same success Young Money garnered in the 2010s. Nicki Minaj is big on working hard and constantly uplifts her fans. She famously quoted:

Barbs stay in school, don't ever complain about hardwork. Work hard it pays off! - Nicki Minaj
